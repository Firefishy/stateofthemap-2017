Conference – August 18 - 20

List of talks and workshops

State of the Map 2017 is taking place from August 18th to 20th 2017 in Aizuwakamatsu, Japan. Thank you for the great responses to our call for proposals and community input! We have completed the selection of talks and will be building of the detailed program shortly. For now check out the list of selected talks and workshops below.
