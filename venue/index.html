Conference – August 18 - 20

Aizu Wakamatsu City Culture Center

Aizuwakamatsu City Culture Center ‐ 14-52, Jotomachi, Aizuwakamatsu, Fukushima

The Aizuwakamatsu City Culture Center is a facility built for various cultural activities. The facility is built on the territory of Tsurugajo castle(As know as Aizuwakamatsu castle) which is a symbol of Fukushima Pref. The castle was the stage of the Boshin war, one of the incident in Japanese history. Immortal castle that endured the fierce attack for a month, and that has sad stories of young samurai team(as know as Byakkotai).

Please also use a printable Offline Guidebook.

Tourist bus

Please get on a tourist bus in front of the Aizuwakamatsu station called "Machinaka Shu-yu Bus(まちなか周遊バス)". Clockwise bus called "Akabe(あかべぇ)", Counterclockwise bus called "High Coller-san(ハイカラさん)", Get off at the bus stop in "Front of the cultural center(文化センター前)", It takes about 25 minutes even if you board either.
